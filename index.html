Central Coast Bikes + Bags is an outdoor campany geared towards the bike-packing community of the Central Coast. Family owned and operated in San Luis Obispo, California since 2020 the company bagan with the intent to provide for the daily adventure lifestyle of the coast. With strong ties from Northern San Fransisco adventure and Southern Santa Barbara outdoor lifestyles, the team has a wide range of backgrounds in both design and adventure.

Our goal is for bike-packing to be more than just a hobby or weekend activity. Central Coast Bikes + Bags is here to support the community and make sure everyone can achieve their adventure trip goals! Have questions or want more information?
